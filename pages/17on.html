Five ways to write haiku

Traditional Haiku

5 - 7 - 5 syllables

An old silent pond

A frog jumps into the pond

Splash! Silence again

- Matsuo Basho

Modern Haiku

4 - 4 - 6 syllables

Autumn moonlight

a worm digs silently

into the chestnut

- Matsuo Basho (adapted)

American Lune

3 - 5 - 3 words

Red leaves fall

silently onto the cold ground

autumn speaks softly

- Contemporary style

Monoku

Single line, ~17 syllables

the longer you gaze at the moon the deeper the silence grows

- Contemporary style

Freeform
